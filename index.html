Landing Pages
One-page websites for products or businesses. Quick to build, easy to sell. £30–£150 each.

Progressive web apps that work on phone. Few people offer this. £150–£600 per project.
Premium

Fix HTML/CSS/JS bugs in existing sites. High demand, quick turnaround. £15–£80 per fix.
Easy entry
How it works
01
Create your listings
On Fiverr you create "Gigs" — set what you offer, price, and delivery time. On Upwork you apply to posted jobs.
02
Get first reviews
Charge slightly less to land your first 5 reviews. Reviews = everything. After 5 good reviews, raise prices.
03
Scale up prices
With 10+ reviews, charge £100–£500 per project. Specialise in one area to charge more.

📦 Dropshipping
sell products online without holding any stock
Overview
Methods
Websites
Notes
📦
How it works: You set up an online store. Customer buys from you. You order from a supplier who ships directly to them. You keep the price difference.
Two models
Classic Dropshipping
Find a product, sell in a Shopify store, supplier ships from AliExpress or a UK supplier. You handle customer service and marketing.
Physical
Dropcoursing
Sell a digital course or guide. No shipping, near 100% margins, fully automated delivery. The course is the product.
DigitalHigh margin
What makes a winning product
Right price range
£15–£80 sweet spot. Too cheap = tiny margin. Too expensive = hard to sell without reviews.
Solves a problem
Products that fix something sell better than ones that just look nice. Pet gadgets, kitchen tools, posture devices.
Trending but not saturated
Use Minea to find products getting ads now but without 100 stores selling them already.
Works in short video
Can you show the product solving a problem in 15 seconds? That's your TikTok ad.

👕 Selling Clothing
print-on-demand — design it, they print and ship it
Overview
Methods
Websites
Notes
👕
How it works: You upload a design. When someone orders, the company prints it on a t-shirt/hoodie and ships it direct to the customer. You never touch the product.
How income works
You set the margin
Platform charges a base cost (e.g. £10/shirt). You set retail price (e.g. £25). You keep £15 profit per sale automatically.
Passive
No stock, no risk
You only pay when someone orders. No upfront stock. No risk of unsold items sitting in a warehouse.
Low risk
Niche designs win
Generic designs don't sell. Target a specific community — dark history fans, gamers, gym people, dog owners.
Strategy
What sells
✓
Simple text-based designs
Minimal typography with a niche phrase. E.g. "I survived the algorithm" for YouTube creators. Easy to make in Canva.
✓
Funny niche humour
Inside jokes for a specific community. Dog owners, programmers, teachers — people share these and buy as gifts.
✓
Aesthetic / minimalist
Clean, stylish designs that look like a real brand. Sells well with good mockup photos.
